I am an undergraduate student at CMU, studying physics and music tech. In my free time, I like to make music, draw, crochet, model, and play softball. If you listen to my music, you will hear an assortment of melancholic, reflective melodies, but I'm also incorporating more jazz elements in my style as well. One day I hope to own a chicken farm and a small recording studio.

I'd say that the music I make reflects my personality pretty accurately - I am reflective, nostalgic, and slightly childish about life. Lastly, I am a curious person and love to approach random strangers to talk about random things, albeit awkwardly. I also tend to think a lot about human nature. My most important values in life are my family, my character, and enjoying the small silly things.
